To stay up to date with us, you can follow our blog, talk to us on IRC, or join our communities on Facebook and Google+.

We also run these mailing lists:

announcements Announcements about upcoming bootcamps and other events. 3-5 messages/month (broadcast only).
discuss The main channel for discussion of direction and technology. 5-20 messages/week
tutors A by-invitation list used to match instructors to boot camps. 5-10 messages/month

These lists are semi-retired:

assessment How to assess what people have learned from us and what impact it's had. sporadic
data For discussion of a new "data carpentry" module. sporadic
funding A by-invitation list for discussion of funding models. sporadic
gits For discussion of our GitHub repositories, website, and workflow. currently inactive
